Where It Began

From the House of Girdhars

The Culinary Canvas is a luxury culinary experience brand curating immersive street food journeys for extraordinary celebrations. Founded by the Girdhar family, who have established an unwavering commitment to creativity, innovation, and transforming catering into a fine art form, the brand creates remarkable experiences utilizing the finest ingredients and cutting-edge techniques.

The Girdhar family has launched "The Culinary Canvas" to celebrate the favorite flavors of regional Indian and global street foods. The Culinary Canvas takes you on an unparalleled cultural culinary voyage, blending bespoke luxury and fun food with its new age molecular gastronomy.

Classic street food in a new avatar is sure to deliver the surprise and tantalize your taste buds. The Culinary Canvas has reinterpreted this culture through a lens of creativity while retaining the authenticity of each dish. The avant-garde brand will make you explore the world's culinary heritage through its theatrical food stations with an exquisite fine dining setup.

The Culinary Facility

State‑of‑the‑Art Infrastructure

Over the years, we have built a stellar reputation based on our honest approach and unwavering commitment to excellence. To support our vision, we have scaled up our infrastructure to create a new state-of-the-art facility.

Our ultra-modern kitchen operates on the cutting edge of International Standards of Food Safety and Quality. Ergonomically designed to maximize productivity and ensure a seamless service flow, the area is well lit, ventilated, and equipped with professional-grade machinery. Specialized storage and cooling chambers preserve the absolute freshness of raw ingredients, backed by a strict Food Safety Management System.

The Pillars

Our Core Philosophy

1. Cultural Honesty

We never dilute regional flavors. Our spice recipes are sourced directly from street side families, executed using authentic techniques like open clay coal pits and iron griddles.

2. Immersive Scenography

Our food stations are works of art. We work with interior designers to design setups incorporating travertine stone, custom clay structures, brass screens, and luxury candlelight layouts.

3. Theatrical Service

Chefs operate as performance artists, finishing dishes in front of guests with culinary showmanship—liquid nitrogen freezing, smoking herbs, and hand-shaving truffles.
