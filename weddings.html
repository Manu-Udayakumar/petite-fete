The Curatorial Marriage

Your Travel Timeline, Cooked Live.

A wedding feast shouldn't be a generic chicken-or-fish banquet. It should reflect your history. If you met in London, had your first date in Delhi, and got engaged in Tokyo, your menu should tell that story.

We design bespoke culinary timelines mapping the couple's travel memoirs. Guests travel through your personal history, tasting the street delicacies that shaped your lives together, refined into a Michelin-inspired visual spectacle.

Scenography

Bespoke Table Landscapes

We coordinate with wedding designers to build dining structures that harmonize with the menu. We design:

• Custom linen napkins stamped with spice profiles.
• Hand-drawn menus printed on deckled cotton paper.
• Atmospheric candlelight curation (over 500 hand-poured taper candles per event).
• Natural floral foliage (like olive branches, eucalyptus, and regional flora) running down long banquet tables.

The Settings

Destination Celebrations

Our teams are globally mobile, operating at private estates and luxury pavilions worldwide.

Palace Grounds

Heritage palatial courtyards in Rajasthan and grand châteaux in France. Live stations are housed within stone arches and lit by historical brass lamps.

Beachfront Villas

Cliffside villas in Ibiza and coastal setups in Amalfi. Modern wood-fired ovens and fresh seafood satays served alongside rolling wave tides.

Orangerie Gardens

Glasshouses in the Cotswolds and historic botanical greenhouses in Paris. Herb-infused cold-press cocktail stations and fresh, clean plating.
