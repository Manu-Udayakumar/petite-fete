Charcoal & Embers

The Binchotan Hearth

Featuring Japanese white oak charcoal (binchotan) burning at extreme, smoke-free heat. Our chefs grill glaze-brushed Otoro tuna, A5 wagyu skewers, and local mushrooms live.

Marble & Gold

The Parisian Crêpe Studio

Set upon slab surfaces of solid Carrara marble. Buckwheat galettes are spun paper-thin, topped with premium sour cream and dollops of salted Royal Oscietra caviar.

Clay & Spices

The Banaras Chaat Gallery

A highly interactive station using hand-thrown organic clay pots. Chefs layer, smash, and season deconstructed street chaat customized to every guest's flavor profile.

Nitrogen & Vapor

The Cryo-Sensory Laboratory

A striking, metallic molecular gastronomy bar. Watch gazpacho spheres, sweet yogurt droplets, and herbal oils flash-frozen instantly in liquid nitrogen vapors.

Fire Clay Wall

The Agra Fort Tandoors

Traditional clay ovens styled with contemporary hammered copper detailing. Flatbreads like saffron sheermal and butter rotis are baked directly on the clay walls and pulled fresh.

Maize & Firewood

The Oaxacan Masa Hearth

Chefs hand-press heirloom blue corn masa and toast it on clay griddles (comals) over oak logs, finishing with melted quesillo cheese, dark mole, and sliced flank steak.
