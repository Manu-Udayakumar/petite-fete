The Master Plan

Curation & Execution

How we transform a conceptual food journey into a sensory reality for your guests. Our meticulous four-step design system.

Phase 01 / Inquiry & Custom Audit

The Culinary Interview

We begin by mapping your history. In our introductory consultation, we don't ask about chicken or fish. We discuss your travel histories, cultural backgrounds, and the specific culinary memories you wish to share with your guests.

Phase 02 / Scenography Rendering

Table & Station Architecture

Our design curators sketch visual layouts of your food stations. We align plate textures (like custom wood slabs, travertine blocks, or hand-fired ceramics), choose matching linens, and arrange architectural candlelight to synchronize with the menu vibe.

Phase 03 / Studio Tasting

The Private Tasting Studio

You are invited to a private gallery session at one of our studios. Here, we present the mockups of your chosen live stations, taste the custom plates cooked live, and align them with curated wine and botanical cocktail pairings.

Phase 04 / Choreographed Setup

Theatrical Execution

On the day of your celebration, our team coordinates with a precision similar to theater. We construct the live station islands, light the binchotan embers, set up molecular mist sprayers, and execute the service with five-star hospitality.
